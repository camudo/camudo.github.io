Most Livable Cities To Live in The World

Article by Travel & Leisure

The world is ever-changing. It has come so far in the past century in terms of resourcefulness.
With this modern age, great urbanization has lead people to cities deemed perfect to live in.
But there are obviously many factors to make a city viable to live in besides job opportunities and generalized idea of "city life".
What specific reasons make these 10 cities the most "liveable" cities in the world - desired by many people who come to them?

  6. Vancouver, Canada (North America)
